About me

Hello! I'm Emma. I recently graduated from KTH Royal Institute of Technology with a M.Sc.Eng in Media Technology.

As an engineer graduate I'd first and foremost refer to myself as a usability enthusiast with leadership experience. With a multitude of interests and a preference to keep learning and challenging myself ‒ I believe it is way too early to box myself into a market defined title just yet.

Product design, service design, interaction, UX and UI are all areas which very much engage me. Not to forget I’d happily throw a little front-end development in there too!

I think kindness cool, have a deep sense of empathy and a lot of integrity. As a result, I’m particularly engaged in creating honest, inclusive and meaningful experiences. Although mostly soft-spoken, a tireless determination and a strong will also makes me unafraid to challenge the status quo.

Skills ‒ UX

Good at sketching and wireframing. Worked with low to high fidelity prototyping. Very comfortable with using Figma and Invision, previous experience of working with Adobe XD.

Capable of working with both Photoshop and InDesign, although I've rarely used them specifically for prototyping.

I can do user interviews, user research, usability testing, personas & scenarios and storyboarding.
I’ve worked with design processes such as Double Diamond and Design Thinking.
