About Emma

I recently graduated from KTH Royal Institute of Technology with a M.Sc.Eng in Media Technology. Don't got a clue what that means? No worries! It's a standing joke among us students that no one knows what we do. I like to use the title UX Engineer, it's at least a little bit better at reflecting what I know.

As an engineer graduate I’d first and foremost refer to myself as a problem solving usability enthusiast with leadership experience. Product design, service design, interaction, UX and UI are all areas which very much engage me. Not to forget I’d happily throw a little frontend development in there too!

I think kindness cool, have a deep sense of empathy and a lot of integrity. Although mostly soft-spoken, a tireless determination and a strong will also makes me unafraid to challenge the status quo.

Skills within UX

Good at sketching and wireframing. Worked with low to high fidelity prototyping. Very comfortable with using Figma and Invision, previous experience of working with Adobe XD.

Capable of working with both Photoshop and InDesign, although I've rarely used them specifically for prototyping.

I can do user interviews, user research, usability testing, personas & scenarios and storyboarding. I’ve worked with design processes such as Double Diamond and Design Thinking.

Skills within Development

Very comfortable with HTML, CSS and JavaScript (+ jQuery). Done a couple of projects with the component library React. Familiar with node.js and I can do information visualisation with D3.

Also comfortable using Python, and have previous experience of working with both SQL and PHP.
