Master Thesis:

Making 5G network data accessible

Together with Telia Company I explored the future of private cellular networks for industry. In this research project on information visualisation I explored 5G networks and the best practices within visualisation to examine how a dashboard tool might be developed. The purpose of the dashboard was to expand the network knowledge beyond the traditional operator, providing industry workers with an easy tool to understand the networks impact in a future digitalised factory.


VIEW CASE  

Interactive Light Forest

This interactive light installation was the final project in the course Physical Interaction Design. The theme of the year was "Designing for the Winter Playground" and our task was to build a piece of playground equipment fit for the harsh, cold and dark winter conditions in Sweden.


VIEW CASE  

CERO Challenge

In Advanced Project Course we did a redesign of the CERO Challenge app for promoting sustainable travel behaviour. With the help of current research into pro-environmental behaviour change, our new version aimed at tying the application closer to its parent organisation, CERO (Climate and Economic Research in Organisations), and new ways to foster sustainable cultures and a social environment focused on encouragement.


VIEW CASE  

Dialogue Tool for Urban Planning

This high-fidelity prototype was developed as a part of the course Cooperative IT-design. The City of Stockholm provided the project challenge; designing a dialogue tool for communication of early stages of urban development. Our result was an application allowing users to explore planned development as 3D-objects layered on top of 360° views.
